Program Opportunities
LU Send Abroad Signature Program: Participating in this unique opportunity allows students to use their Liberty scholarships and take special Liberty courses while studying abroad for a semester or summer. Students have the opportunity to dive deeper into their host culture through academic or service internships.

LU Send Abroad Custom Programs: Students are provided incredible opportunities to study and Intern abroad. These programs are diverse in location, academic focus allowing for a sense of customization. Custom Programs provide:
  1. Opportunities to take courses at host universities and study centers.
  2. Opportunities to add an Internship to their study abroad experience.
  3. Opportunities to participate exclusively in summer internships.
LU Send Abroad Independent Internships: Participating in these programs provide tangible ways to gain hands on professional experience in a cross culturally rich environments. Students will work alongside and learn from specialists within their field of study. These programs are exclusively for students looking for internship opportunities.
