"Join us in Ecuador as we gain a better understanding of the challenges this country faces in anti-human trafficking efforts and how organizations are working to meet these challenges. Learn about the dynamics of legalized prostitution and its impact on human trafficking and gain knowledge of the prevention, protection, and partnership work being developed. Team members will have a variety of opportunities to be immersed in the culture (church services, market places, and the Galapagos Islands) while being exposed to ministry efforts in prison ministries, home visits, safe houses, and brothel areas. This trip will satisfy the requirements for PSYC 499 internship."
