Liberty University values the safety of its students. Considering this fact, the University has deemed specific countries unsafe for travel.
For more information on which countries are restricted, please speak to your International Internship Coordinator.
Partner Programs These partner organizations have a direct billing agreement with Liberty University. The invoice for your internship program is sent to Liberty University and posted to your Student Account. Applicable Financial Aid and Institutional aid may be utilized to pay for your courses and portions of the program cost if completed during the Fall or Spring semesters. To find out more regarding applicable Financial Aid please communicate with your International Internship Coordinator.
